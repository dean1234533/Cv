About Your Personal Trainer: Dean Burt

About Dean

REPs Certified Personal Trainer

I'm Dean — a REPs certified personal trainer based in East London. Qualified since 2019, my focus is on helping people build real strength, lose weight and feel genuinely confident in their own body. Every session is structured, purposeful and built around your individual goals — not a one-size-fits-all template.

Training the Mind & Body

Fitness isn't just physical — it's about building discipline, consistency and belief in yourself. I train each client as an individual, adapting every session to their goals, current level and lifestyle. You'll always know why you're doing what you're doing, and you'll see the results to prove it.
