Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the training sessions take place?

Sessions are outdoors across East London — including Ilford, Stratford, Wanstead, Goodmayes and Romford — using local parks and outdoor gym spaces. Home-based sessions can also be arranged to suit you.

How long is each session?

Every session is 60 minutes and fully structured — a proper warm-up, a focused main workout tailored to your goals, and a cool-down to finish safely.

Do I need a gym or my own equipment?

No gym required. Training uses park spaces, outdoor gym equipment and effective bodyweight exercises, so you can get a full workout without a membership.

Do you offer group or pairs training?

Yes — as well as one-to-one personal training, group and pairs sessions are available if you'd prefer to train with a friend or partner.

Are there any discounts available?

Yes. Key workers and NHS staff receive an additional 14% off all block bookings, and students get an additional 10% off blocks of 10 and 15 sessions. Get in touch to find out more.

How do I get started?

Simply book a free, no-commitment consultation. We'll talk through your goals and find the best plan for you before you decide anything.
