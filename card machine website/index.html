How We Handle Your Information

This policy explains how your personal information is collected and used when you submit an enquiry through this website.

Who Collects Your Information

This form is operated by LGG Trading Ltd, a lead generation service. LGG Trading Ltd collects your information and passes it to Payment Card Services, who will then contact you about your enquiry.

LGG Trading Ltd is the data controller at the point of collection. Once your information is passed to Payment Card Services, they become the data controller for all communications and services relating to your enquiry.

What We Collect

The information you provide on the form, including your name, contact details, location, and any details about your enquiry.

Why We Collect It (Lawful Basis)

We process your personal data on the basis of legitimate interests, specifically to respond to your enquiry and pass it to Payment Card Services so they can contact you about the services you've asked about.

Where we send you marketing beyond your specific enquiry, we rely on your consent and you can withdraw that consent at any time.

Who We Share Your Information With

Your information is shared with Payment Card Services within 48 working hours of submission. Payment Card Services provides the following services: Card Machine and Merchant Payment Services.

LGG Trading Ltd does not contact you directly about services. All service-related contact comes from Payment Card Services.

We do not sell your information to third parties.

How Long We Keep Your Information

LGG Trading Ltd retains your information for up to 6 years from your last interaction with us. This period reflects the statutory limitation period for contractual and regulatory matters under UK law. After this period, your data is securely deleted or anonymised.

Payment Card Services retains your information in line with their own retention policy, generally up to 6 years from your last interaction for the same reasons.

If you ask us to delete your information earlier, we will do so unless we have a legal obligation to keep it.

International Transfers

Your data is processed and stored within the UK. If at any point your data is transferred outside the UK or EEA, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the UK Information Commissioner.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where consent is the lawful basis)
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact LGG Trading Ltd or Payment Card Services using the details below.

Opting Out

You can opt out of further communications at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any SMS
  • Clicking unsubscribe in any email
  • Contacting Payment Card Services directly

Data Security
