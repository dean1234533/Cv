FAQ

Common questions

Who is MindFlow for?

Anyone who has meetings, bookings, clients, or just a lot to keep track of. Freelancers, personal trainers, coaches, consultants, small business owners — or anyone who wants their tasks, notes and schedule in one organised place.

How do the booking links work?

You connect your Google Calendar and set the times you want to offer. MindFlow generates a shareable link. Visitors only see genuinely free slots — nothing that clashes with your existing calendar events. No double bookings, no back-and-forth.

Does it work on my phone?

Yes. MindFlow is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — install it on your iPhone or Android home screen like a native app. It also works fully offline, so your notes and tasks are always available even without signal.

What's included in Pro?

Pro gives you everything in Starter plus unlimited booking links, the AI smart agent, voice recording and transcription, full contact and meeting management, push notifications, and priority support.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel anytime with no penalty. Your subscription runs to the end of the billing period and you won't be charged again. All payments are handled securely via Stripe.
